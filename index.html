About Me

About Me

Martin Morales was raised in a small suburban town called Plano, IL. He is currently a senior student studying computer science and math at Dominican University, River Forest, IL. He has held several unique jobs with roles mandating subsets of various skills ranging from customer service, equipment management, goal-orientation, time management, and communication. His passion for computers and software development has developed a natural fit in the ever-growing industry of technology through assisting his church community in designing a website, developing several skills through his work-study job with the Information Technology Department at Dominican University, and personal experience in developing software. In his free time, Martin loves to play guitar, exercise with friends, and learn more about software development. Grateful for his friends and family gained through his participating communities, Martin looks forward to finding his career with strong encouragement.
